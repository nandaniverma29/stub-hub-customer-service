How do Consumers Feel About Calling Stubhub Customer Service?

Stubhub has historically had customer relations problems, primarily due to the company adding additional fees to ticket prices that were not always obvious at the beginning of the purchase process.

Another issue is that since Stubhub is a third-party business that does not actually own the venues it sells tickets for, the company has limited power when it comes to addressing problems with a specific venue.

What Kind of Issues can Stubhub Customer Service Representative Resolve?

Stubhub customer service representatives can resolve a range of issues, including questions about tickets and ticket delivery, issues surrounding credit card charges and ticket pricing, and the possibility that a ticket was issued for the wrong class of seating.

Customer support agents can also, when possible, assist with ticket changes and refunds.

What Can't Be Resolved With a Call to Stubhub Customer Service?

Venues set their own policies on some issues, such as seating and age-restrictions: Stubhub isn't able to intervene if a customer purchases tickets to a show without understanding these policies. In many cases, tickets are non-refundable and Stubhub is not authorized to initiate refunds to purchasers.

Individuals who purchase tickets issued by Stubhub through a third party, such as a "scalper" or someone who sells tickets on eBay or Facebook, often have no protection against fraud. Stubhub is not obligated to assist individuals who purchase a ticket through an unauthorized seller.

What Should You Do If You Have an Unsuccessful Call with Stubhub?
  • If you've tried resolving an issue or getting an answer to a question by calling Stubhub and yet hung up the phone feeling dissatisfied, don't give up. You may still have options.
  • First, review any notes that you took during your original call. If you don't have any notes, try and recall what happened during your conversation and write it down. This information can be very helpful if you have to escalate your case.
  • Next, call Stubhub back. The next representative you speak to may be better able to assist you.
  • If a second call does not resolve your problem, consider getting in touch with Stubhub through a different method. If you have already purchased your ticket, log into your account online and request a live chat. Other options include getting in touch via email or through social media. All of these options have the advantage of providing you with a written transcript of your conversation.
  • If Stubhub remains unresponsive, Try sending a letter through postal mail. Be sure to request tracking and delivery confirmation. This is not a fast option, but it does show Stubhub that you are serious about this issue and your letter may end up in the hands of somebody who will be able to take the time to read it and take effective action on your case.
